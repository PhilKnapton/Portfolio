### Community Computer Refurbishment – Literacy Central Vancouver Island

### Overview
This project involved volunteering with Literacy Central Vancouver Island to help refurbish and prepare donated computers for redistribution within the community. The goal was to ensure that individuals and families have access to reliable technology for education, communication, and everyday digital needs.

### Responsibilities
- Refurbishing donated desktop and laptop computers
- Installing and configuring operating systems
- Performing hardware checks and basic repairs
- Preparing systems for community distribution

### Purpose
Access to technology is essential for education, job searching, and communication. By refurbishing donated computers, this initiative helps reduce electronic waste while providing functional systems to individuals who may not otherwise have access to reliable devices.

### Skills Demonstrated
- Computer hardware troubleshooting
- Operating system installation and configuration
- System preparation for end users
- Community-focused technology support
